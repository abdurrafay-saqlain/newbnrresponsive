How Does BnR360 ERP Improve Staffing and Recruiting Techniques ?

Staffing and recruiting is the heart of your company; responsible for finding, hiring, training, and supporting new employees. Here’s how BnR360 can help you do more:

1- Centralizing your data base: Your organization must have a centralized system for managing and maintaining data that can be used throughout. A unified view of all the data is provided by an ERP system, which consolidates employee’s data, including their name, work history, performance review, and other crucial details. An ERP can also help quickly retrieve this data.

2- Improving reporting: Help your organization keep track of important elements of business operations with the help of role-based reporting. Make intelligent decisions by custom reports the ERP produces and draw attention to or improve areas related to human resources. Streamline the entire reporting process by creating specific reports for each role in the company and save time by giving only the precise information required.

3- Streamline recruiting: BnR360 benefits each phase of your recruitment process. In order to have a productive hiring process and an effective recruitment system, organizations must be prepared to build relationships with clients and applicants, which can be a very time-consuming and difficult process. BnR360 simplifies the process. Easily organize interviews and sort resumes, use keyword-based filtering within the software and make recruitment easier than ever.

4- Workload Management: Use BnR360 to lighten workload. Easily track project progress, completion rates, overtime, and the availability and unavailability of human resources through workload and resource management. Systematically keep track of the resources and procedures employed in a project, the stages of a company's growth in terms of work, and employee performance feedback.

5- Payroll Management: Regardless of the number of employees, managing employee payroll is a crucial part of any firm. It can be quite difficult and time-consuming to determine each employee's pay and additional benefits, such as health, insurance, and loans. Mistakes might endanger the organization severely. However, this process is made simple and stress-free with BnR360. The active tracking of payments, rewards, loans, insurance, and deductions, when necessary, is made automated and hassle-free.
