Frequently Asked Questions

What is BnR360 Staffing Services ?
BnR360 staffing services is a professional service that helps businesses find, recruit, and hire qualified employees on a temporary, temp-to-perm or permanent basis.
How Does BnR360 Staffing Services Benefit My Business ?
BnR360 staffing services can help businesses save time and money by taking care of the recruitment and hiring process. We also provide access to a larger pool of qualified candidates and provide flexibility to meet the changing needs of the business.
HOW DOES THE ONBOARDING PROCESS WORK WITH BnR360 STAFFING SERVICE ?
The onboarding process with BnR360 staffing service typically begins with an initial consultation where the staffing agency will understand your business needs and the type of positions you need to fill. The agency will then present a pool of qualified candidates for you to review, interview, and select from. Once a candidate is selected, the agency will handle the hiring processes.
HOW MUCH DO BnR360 STAFFING SERVICES COST ?
The cost of BnR360 staffing services vary depending on the type of staffing service you choose. There are four services to choose from. We have a fixed monthly subscription plan for each service. Contact us to know more about the subscribtion plan.
